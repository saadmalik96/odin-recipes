Bacon Egg and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 thick strips of bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • A slice of cheese
  • Brioche buns
  • Some butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Chilli Powder

Steps

  1. Coat a pan with butter
  2. Place bacon in a pan, add water and start heating it up until water boils off
  3. Mix eggs, salt, pepper and chilli powder in a bowl and add to pan with butter
  4. When eggs are almost cooked add a slice of cheese in the center and fold eggs over cheese
  5. Take eggs off the pan and toast brioche buns
  6. After water boils off flip bacon occasionally til nice and crispy on both sides
  7. Construct BEC Sandwich and immediately wrap in aluminum foil
