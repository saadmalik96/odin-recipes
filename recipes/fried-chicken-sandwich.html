Fried Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sauce
    • Mustard - 1 part
    • Ketchup - 2 parts
    • Mayo - 2 parts
    • Paprika
    • Onion Powder
  • Dredge
    • Flour - 50 grams
    • Cornstarch - 25 grams
    • Black Pepper - 20 cranks
    • Salt
    • Red Chilli Powder
    • Smoked Paprika
    • Garlic Powder
    • Oregano
  • Chicken
    • 2 Chicken Thighs
    • Milk
    • Vinegar
    • Salt - A pinch
  • Other essential components
    • Brioche buns
    • Lettuce
    • Pickled Onions
    • Tomatoes
    • Sweet Pickles

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a bowl and add milk, vinegar and a pinch of salt
  2. Mix all ingredients for the dregde
  3. Fire up stove and start heating up a saucepan filled with vegetable oil
  4. Drop some chicken mixture into the dredge to form small clumps
  5. Add chicken to dredge and coat generously before deep frying for 10-12 minutes
  6. Prep other essential components and sauce while the chicken fries.
  7. Prepare the burger when chicken is completely cooked
  8. Enjoy!
