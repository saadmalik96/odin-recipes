Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

  • Kung Pao Sauce
    • Brown Sugar - 33 grams
    • Corn Starch - 10 grams
    • Soy Sauce - 60 grams
    • Chinese Black Vinegar - 50 grams
    • Chicken Stock - 44 grams
    • Water - 36 grams
    • Shaoxing Wine - 31 grams
  • Chicken
    • Chicken Thighs - 2lbs
    • Corn Starch - 4 grams
    • Soy Sauce - 36 grams
    • Shaoxing Wine - 10 grams
  • Other essential components
    • Vegetable oil - 4 tbsp
    • Raw Peanuts - 50 grams
    • Tianjin Chillis - 10 chillies
    • Green Onions
    • Garlic
    • Ginger

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces and add to a bowl
  2. Add soy sauce, shaoxing wine and corn starch and mix thoroughly. Leave to rest for 30 mins.
  3. Prepare Kung Pao Sauce, chop green onions and mesasure peanuts and chillies
  4. Add Vegetable Oil to wok, followed by peanuts and chillies respectively
  5. Remove peanuts and chillies and cook chicken in batches and then remove all chicken
  6. Add Green onions, garlic and ginger and cook for 30 seconds
  7. Add back chicken, peanuts, chillies and kung pao and cook til sauce as reduced to a thick black sauce
  8. Serve with rice and enjoy!
